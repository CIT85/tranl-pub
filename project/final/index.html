My routine

Step Product Description
1 Cleanser Gently cleanse your face with a suitable cleanser for your skin type. Use lukewarm water and massage in circular motions.
2 Exfoliator Apply an exfoliating product to remove dead skin cells and unclog pores. Use 1-2 times a week to avoid over-exfoliation.
3 Toner Apply toner to balance your skin's pH levels and remove any remaining impurities. Look for alcohol-free formulas.
4 Serum Apply a serum with active ingredients like vitamin C or hyaluronic acid to target specific skin concerns such as dark spots or fine lines.
5 Moisturizer Hydrate your skin with a moisturizer suitable for your skin type. Choose a lightweight formula for oily skin and a richer formula for dry skin.
6 Sunscreen Apply sunscreen with at least SPF 30 every morning to protect your skin from UV rays and prevent premature aging and skin damage.
7 Eye Cream Gently apply eye cream to the under-eye area to hydrate, reduce puffiness, and minimize the appearance of dark circles.
8 Face Mask Use a face mask 1-2 times a week for additional skincare benefits such as deep cleansing, hydration, or targeted treatment.
