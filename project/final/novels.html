Manhua

Chinese Comic

"Manhua" is a style of Chinese comic art that encompasses a wide range of genres, from action-packed martial arts adventures to heartwarming romance stories. These comics are typically read from left to right, similar to Western comics, and often feature vibrant artwork, expressive characters, and engaging storylines that captivate readers of all ages.

Manhwa

Korean Comic

"Manwha"Manhwa refers to Korean comics or graphic novels that cover various genres such as fantasy, romance, action, and slice of life. These stories are typically read from left to right, similar to Western comics, and are known for their colorful artwork, intricate plots, and well-developed characters. Manhwa has gained popularity globally due to its diverse range of themes and engaging storytelling techniques.

Manga

Japanese Comic

"Manga" Manga is a style of Japanese comic books and graphic novels that cover a wide range of genres, including action, romance, fantasy, science fiction, and more. These comics are typically read from right to left, starting from the back of the book in traditional Japanese style. Manga is known for its distinctive art style, exaggerated expressions, and detailed storytelling, making it a popular medium both in Japan and internationally.
